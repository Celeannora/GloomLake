## How to find a card

**Pattern**: `C:\Temp\GloomLake\assets\data\cards_by_category/{type}/{type}_{first_letter}.csv`

**Example**: Atraxa (creature, A) -> `C:\Temp\GloomLake\assets\data\cards_by_category/creature/creature_a.csv`
